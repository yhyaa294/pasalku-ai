# Product Overview

Pasalku.ai is an AI-powered legal consultation platform designed specifically for Indonesian law. It's a comprehensive legal technology solution that combines dual AI systems (BytePlus Ark + Groq) to provide accurate, fast, and reliable legal solutions.

## Core Features

- **AI Legal Consultation**: Instant legal advice with 94.1% accuracy using dual AI consensus
- **Document Analysis**: Automated OCR and deep analysis for contracts and legal documents  
- **Knowledge Graph**: Comprehensive Indonesian legal database with semantic search
- **Professional Verification**: System for verified legal practitioners with premium features
- **Multi-language Support**: Full support for Indonesian and English

## Target Users

- Individual users seeking legal guidance
- Legal professionals and practitioners
- Businesses requiring contract analysis
- Students and researchers in legal field

## Business Model

- Freemium model with tiered subscriptions (Free, Premium Rp 99K/month, Professional Rp 397K/month)
- Usage-based pricing for enterprise clients
- Professional verification system for enhanced credibility

## Key Differentiators

- First AI legal platform specifically for Indonesian law
- Dual AI system for enhanced accuracy
- Enterprise-grade security (PDPA compliant)
- Comprehensive 96+ AI features
- Multi-database architecture for optimal performance
